StreetMeetWA builds on the foundation established in Washington, D.C. Based in Seattle, the community is led by Kristina — a digital creator, movement artist, and visual storyteller committed to fostering genuine connection among creatives. Follow StreetMeetWA on Instagram for upcoming events and community updates.

Kristina
@kk_in_motion

Kristina is a digital creator, movement artist, and visual storyteller who blends media and presence into her work. With a mission to help women feel confident in who they are, she creates spaces both online and in person where self expression, creativity, and growth can thrive.

As the host of StreetMeetWA in Seattle, she brings a refined creative eye and grounded, expressive energy to the community, fostering an environment where photographers, models, and creatives can connect, collaborate, and create freely.

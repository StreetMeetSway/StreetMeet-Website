StreetMeet MD is our latest community. Led by Rafael Ortegano, Ned MacFadden, and Malik, the community connects photographers, models, and visual artists from Baltimore to Annapolis and the suburbs of the DMV.

Follow StreetMeetMD on Instagram for upcoming events and updates.

Community Hosts
Rafael Ortegano
@rafortex

Rafael is a Baltimore-based photographer and filmmaker from Argentina. With nearly a decade of experience in visual storytelling, he focuses on capturing real moments and turning them into strong, cinematic narratives.

Learn more about Rafael →
Ned MacFadden
@nedsphotons

Ned MacFadden is a Baltimore-born portrait photographer, videographer, and educator whose work is grounded in curiosity and connection. He teaches Media Production at Eastern Middle School in Silver Spring, Maryland, and has spent over a decade working with Passion 4 Learning to build digital literacy skills in underserved communities. Whether behind the camera or in the classroom, Ned is drawn to the stories people carry — and finding the right way to tell them.

Malik
@sappworld

Malik is a designer and photographer based in Silver Spring, MD. His work engages culture, community, and creative possibility through bold graphics and an animated visual vocabulary. His photography practice is intuitive and narrative-driven, balancing research and instinct to tell stories and capture form.

StreetMeet DC has served as the original home of StreetMeet — and over more than a decade, it has grown into one of the DMV's most active communities for photographers, models, and visual storytellers. Today, the community is led by photographer and visual artist Kevin Wayne and multidisciplinary artist DeVante Capers. We invite you to be part of the community.

Stay up to date on events and announcements by following StreetMeetDC on Instagram.

Community Hosts
Kevin Wayne
@k3vin.wayne

Kevin Wayne is a Washington, D.C.–born photographer and visual storyteller with over 15 years of experience behind the camera. Growing up in the city has deeply shaped his perspective—informing the way he observes people, spaces, and the quiet rhythms of city life.

DeVante Capers
@devantecapers92

DeVante is a multidisciplinary artist whose work explores what it means to move through the world as an outsider. Diagnosed with high-functioning autism, he uses painting, writing, dance, photography, and mixed media to translate his lived experiences into visual and embodied stories.
